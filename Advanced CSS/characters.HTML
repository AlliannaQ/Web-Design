Bluey Characters

Bluey

Age: 6

Bluey is a spirited and imaginative Blue Heeler pup with a boundless curiosity for the world around her. Whether she's embarking on epic adventures or solving everyday challenges, Bluey’s playful nature and cleverness make her a natural leader and an inspiring role model for young viewers. Her creativity and enthusiasm shine through in every game she plays.

Bingo

Age: 4

Bingo is Bluey’s sweet and gentle younger sister. She may be smaller, but her heart is huge. With a vivid imagination and a love for fun, Bingo often finds herself joining Bluey on adventures or discovering new ways to play. Her warm personality and affectionate nature make her a beloved character in the Bluey family.

Bandit

Age: 36

Bandit is Bluey and Bingo’s dad, known for his playful spirit and loving guidance. As an enthusiastic father, he often takes on various roles in his children’s imaginative games, from a pirate to a superhero. Bandit’s sense of humor and patient support help make every day an adventure for his family.

Chili

Age: 34

Chilli is Bluey and Bingo’s mum, who balances her role as a nurturing mother with a fun-loving approach to family life. She’s known for her practicality, warmth, and ability to join in on games with just the right mix of fun and wisdom. Chilli’s supportive nature and caring attitude help guide her family through all of life’s ups and downs.
